Videography Short Film

Äpfel im Mai

Screenwriter / Director / Editor

An award-winning short film exploring themes through strictly taught class vocabulary, winning the Golden Boar Award.

Project Overview

This section provides a high-level summary of the project goals. For "Äpfel im Mai," the challenge was to tell a compelling story using only the specific German vocabulary taught in the GER102 course.

My Role

I led the development from concept to final cut. This included the entire pipeline of production.

  • Creative Direction: Planning the visual tone and narrative structure.
  • Scriptwriting: Crafting the dialogue around course-specific constraints.
  • Editing: Handling the pacing and color grading to build a clear viewer experience.

Process & Thinking

Describe your approach to the creative challenge. How did you think through the limitations?.

Similar to documenting how the rink feels like home, I focused on creating an immersive portrait through specific visual atmospheric choices.

Outcome

The final project strengthened my understanding of storytelling hierarchy and intentional design. It culminated in receiving the Golden Boar Award at the University of Waterloo.
